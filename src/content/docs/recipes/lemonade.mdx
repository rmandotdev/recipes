## Instructions

1. Slice the lemons.
2. Put the lemons, sugar and water in a large pot.
3. Bring to a boil.
4. Turn off the heat.
5. Wait 15-20 minutes.
6. Pour the lemonade into different containers or simply remove the lemons.
7. Wait until it cools down to room temperature.
8. Place in the fridge and wait until it is cold.
