## Recipe

### In a Multi-cooker

If you have a multi-cooker, the recipe is simple:

<RecipeSteps
  steps={[
    text("Pour the buckwheat into the multi-cooker"),
    text("Add the water and salt"),
    text("Let it cook for 15-20 minutes"),
  ]}
/>

### In a Pot

<RecipeSteps
  steps={[
    text("Pour the water into a pot and bring to a boil"),
    text("Reduce the heat to low"),
    text("Pour the buckwheat into the pot"),
    text("Add the salt"),
    text("Cover the pot tightly with a lid"),
    text("Cook for 15-20 minutes without opening the lid"),
    text("Turn off the heat"),
    text("Let the buckwheat rest under the lid for another 5-10 minutes"),
  ]}
/>
