## Recipe

### In a Multi-cooker

If you have a multi-cooker, the recipe is simple:

1. Pour the buckwheat into the multi-cooker.
2. Add the water and salt.
3. Let it cook for 15-20 minutes.

### In a Pot

1. Pour the water into a pot and bring to a boil.
2. Reduce the heat to low.
3. Pour the buckwheat into the pot.
4. Add the salt.
5. Cover the pot tightly with a lid.
6. Cook for 15-20 minutes without opening the lid.
7. Turn off the heat.
8. Let the buckwheat rest under the lid for another 5-10 minutes.
