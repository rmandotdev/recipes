## Instructions

1. Put the chicken thighs in a pot with the water. Bring to a boil.
2. Skim off any foam.
3. Add the bouillon cubes.
4. Simmer for 30-40 minutes until the chicken is cooked through.
5. Remove the chicken thighs. Shred the meat and return it to the pot.
6. Add the noodles and cook for 5-7 minutes until tender.
7. Serve hot.
