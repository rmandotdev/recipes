## Instructions

1. Peel and chop the carrots, potatoes, and onions.
2. Place the smoked ribs in a pot and cover with water.
3. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat, cover the pot with a lid, and simmer for 1 hour.
4. While the ribs are simmering, sauté the onions and carrots in a pan until golden.
5. Add the peas and the sautéed onions and carrots to the pot; simmer for another hour.
6. Add the potatoes and simmer for a final 30 minutes until tender.
